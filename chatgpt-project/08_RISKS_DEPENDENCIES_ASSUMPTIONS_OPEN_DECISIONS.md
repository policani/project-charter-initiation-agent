# Risks, Dependencies, Assumptions, and Open Decisions

## Risks

Uncertain events that could affect outcomes. Capture cause, impact, likelihood, mitigation direction, and owner.

## Dependencies

External conditions, teams, vendors, decisions, environments, or systems required for success. Capture owner and needed date.

## Assumptions

Statements treated as true for planning but not yet proven. Every material assumption should either be confirmed, converted into a dependency, or tracked as a risk.

## Open decisions

Questions requiring a named decision maker. Track decision owner, due date, options, and impact of delay.

## Rule

Do not bury unresolved items inside narrative text. Put them in visible tables so sponsors and governance bodies can act on them.
