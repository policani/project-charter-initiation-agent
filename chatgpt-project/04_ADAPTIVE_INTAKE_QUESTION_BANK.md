# Adaptive Intake Question Bank

Ask only the highest-value missing questions.

## Authorization

- Who is the executive sponsor?
- Who owns the business outcome?
- Has funding or capacity been approved?
- What decision body authorizes initiation?

## Business outcome

- What business outcome does this project support?
- Why now?
- What happens if nothing changes?
- Which metric or operational condition should improve?

## Scope

- What is explicitly in scope?
- What is explicitly out of scope?
- What is the minimum viable project boundary?
- What common stakeholder assumption must be excluded?

## Deliverables

- What deliverables are authorized?
- Which deliverables are required for closure?
- Which outputs are planning artifacts rather than project deliverables?

## Constraints

- What budget, timeline, policy, regulatory, technology, resource, vendor, or operational constraints apply?
- Are there blackout windows, release windows, or immovable deadlines?

## Governance

- Who makes scope, funding, timeline, and priority decisions?
- What issues require escalation?
- What change-control threshold applies?

## Success

- What measurable success conditions will be used?
- What adoption, operational, financial, compliance, customer, or technical outcomes matter?
