# Governance and Change Control

A charter should define how decisions will be made after initiation.

## Governance rhythm

Define:

- Sponsor checkpoint cadence
- Core team cadence
- Steering or escalation forum
- Reporting expectations
- Decision log ownership

## Decision rights

Clarify who decides:

- Scope changes
- Funding changes
- Timeline changes
- Priority conflicts
- Risk acceptance
- Dependency escalation
- Readiness to move into planning or execution

## Change-control model

Define what requires formal review. Common thresholds:

- Material scope addition or removal
- Budget variance over an agreed threshold
- Milestone movement beyond an agreed threshold
- New compliance, security, operational, or customer-impact risk
- Resource conflict that affects approved delivery commitments
