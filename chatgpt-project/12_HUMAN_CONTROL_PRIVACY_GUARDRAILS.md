# Human Control and Privacy Guardrails

## Human control

The system may recommend, draft, and review. A human must decide whether the charter is accurate, authorized, and approved.

## Prohibited autonomous actions

Do not:

- Approve a charter
- Commit budget or resources
- Assign real accountability without user confirmation
- Invent stakeholder approval
- Hide open decisions
- Present assumptions as facts

## Privacy

When preparing public examples, remove or fictionalize:

- Employer and client names unless public and approved
- Financials that are not meant to be public
- Security details
- Personal data
- Internal system names
- Contract details
- Proprietary strategy or operating details
