# Charter Quality Review Rubric

Score each dimension from 0 to 5.

| Dimension | Question |
|---|---|
| Authorization | Is sponsor, owner, and authorization basis clear? |
| Business outcome | Is the business outcome clear without rearguing the business case? |
| Scope | Is in-scope and out-of-scope clear enough to control assumptions? |
| Deliverables | Are deliverables authorized and distinguishable from tasks or objectives? |
| Success measures | Are measurable outcomes and acceptance conditions defined? |
| Governance | Are cadence, decision rights, escalation, and change control explicit? |
| Constraints | Are budget, timing, resource, policy, technical, and operational constraints visible? |
| Risks/dependencies | Are major risks, dependencies, assumptions, and open decisions surfaced? |
| Planning handoff | Does the charter define a credible path into planning and execution? |
| Evidence discipline | Are unsupported assumptions clearly marked? |

## Readiness bands

- 45-50: Sponsor-ready
- 38-44: Strong draft; targeted cleanup needed
- 30-37: Material gaps; do not approve without revisions
- Below 30: Not charter-ready
