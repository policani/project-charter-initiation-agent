# Delivery Plan Excerpt

## Proposed delivery approach

Hybrid delivery model:

- Initiation and planning: structured governance and scope baseline
- Dashboard and operating model design: iterative validation with pilot teams
- Configuration/build: short delivery cycles with weekly business review
- Pilot execution: weekly exception review and adoption tracking
- Handoff: runbook and support model transition to Onboarding Operations

## Planning artifacts needed after charter approval

- Work breakdown and milestone plan
- Data profiling report
- Security review plan
- Pilot cohort selection
- Risk and dependency register
- Communications plan
- Operational handoff checklist
