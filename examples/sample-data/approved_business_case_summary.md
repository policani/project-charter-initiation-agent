# Approved Business Case Summary: Customer Onboarding Visibility

## Background

Enterprise onboarding work is tracked across CRM, spreadsheets, implementation trackers, and support readiness notes. Leadership has approved a pilot investment to improve visibility and reduce avoidable handoff delay.

## Approved outcome

Improve onboarding predictability for enterprise customers by establishing shared status, blocker, dependency, and launch-readiness visibility across Sales, Implementation, Customer Success, Finance, and Support.

## Value hypothesis

The approved business case expects reduced manual status reconciliation, earlier escalation of blockers, and improved schedule predictability for enterprise onboarding projects.

## Investment envelope

Pilot investment approved up to $425,000. Expansion beyond pilot requires steering approval.

## Charter instruction

The charter should reference this business case but should not restate the entire value argument.
