## Purpose

The project exists because enterprise onboarding status is fragmented across multiple tools and manual updates. The approved business case supports improving onboarding predictability and reducing manual coordination burden.

## Scope clarification

The first phase is a North America Enterprise pilot. It is not a full onboarding transformation and does not replace CRM, the implementation tracker, or the enterprise analytics platform.

## Success clarification

The sponsor cares about predictability, earlier blocker visibility, reduced manual reconciliation, and pilot adoption of a standard status taxonomy.

## Governance clarification

Biweekly steering is sufficient during planning and build. Weekly exception review is required during pilot execution. Sponsor or steering approval is required for scope expansion, budget exception, launch commitment changes, or new security/compliance risk.
