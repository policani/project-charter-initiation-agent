# Charter Quality Review: Customer Onboarding Control Tower Modernization

## Overall readiness recommendation

**Conditionally initiation-ready.** The sample charter is strong enough for sponsor review and planning authorization. The remaining items are normal planning-entry decisions rather than fatal charter defects.

**Weighted readiness estimate:** 4.7 / 5.0

## Fatal defects

None.

## Material gaps to resolve during planning

1. Confirm pilot cohort and number of active onboarding projects.
2. Confirm customer-name display versus anonymized account ID approach.
3. Establish baseline for cycle-time variance and manual reconciliation effort.
4. Confirm data access permissions and security review path.
5. Replace group-level dependency owners with named accountable owners where required.

## Sponsor approval risk

Low, assuming the sponsor agrees that this phase is a bounded pilot and not a full onboarding transformation.

## Critical guidance note

The strongest governance choice in this charter is the explicit exclusion of CRM replacement, full onboarding redesign, and enterprise analytics platform build. Without those exclusions, the project would likely expand beyond the approved pilot envelope.
