# Charter Quality Rubric

A sponsor-ready charter should be judged on authorization clarity, business outcome linkage, scope control, deliverable clarity, success measures, governance discipline, constraints, risks, dependencies, assumptions, open decisions, and planning handoff readiness.

## Scoring

Use a 0-5 score for each dimension.

- 5 = clear, specific, evidence-supported, and actionable
- 4 = strong with minor cleanup needed
- 3 = usable but contains material ambiguity
- 2 = weak and likely to cause execution confusion
- 1 = mostly absent or unsupported
- 0 = not addressed

## Readiness bands

- 45-50: Sponsor-ready
- 38-44: Strong draft; targeted cleanup needed
- 30-37: Material gaps; do not approve without revisions
- Below 30: Not charter-ready
